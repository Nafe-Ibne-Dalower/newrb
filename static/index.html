Our Goals

Checkout our Goals

Awaring RBM Community

First we will aware RBM community of climate actions. Being a leading school our climate activist team wants to be change maker. If we could fulfill this goal we hope that we will be able to fulfill our other goals very easily.

Connecting with Schools

We have a goal to spread our activity to neighboring schools. As students are the future generation. If we can make students aware this activity will materialize successfully. So, we have set a goal to connect with our neighboring schools.

Less Carbon More Oxygen

World Carbon emissions for 2019 were 34,344,006.07 Kilotons of Carbon-dioxide which will cause great harm to Bio-diversity. We cherish the aim of lessening the world from this carbon emission.

Missions

Missions to achieve less Carbon & More Oxygen

Tree Plantation

As we have a goal of less carbon emission tree plantation is very important to us. Since 2017 we have started 100 palm seeds plantations. We hope that tree plantation will reduce the level of Carbon-di-oxide.

Rooftop Garden

For urbanization now we have less amount of fields for planting trees. So, we have taken the goal of creating "Rooftop garden". It will not only help the Climate but also help a man if he wants to use it as an economic source. We hope that it will ensure a green world for us.

Let's work together

We are working relentlessly to ensure a safe world for the new generation. It is not an optional duty rather we should do it for our own sake because if the earth destroys there is no question that our existance will be impossible.

Saving Turag

Turag, the upper tributary of the Buriganga, is a major river in Bangladesh. Because of many causes, it is being polluted. We have alraedy taken initiatives to save it from pollution as it is a great asset to our country & the Bio-diversity. We designed how we will save it. We arranged a zoom meeting when it was time of Covid-19.

Planting Hundred Palm Seeds

We have also taken a program to plant hundred Palm Seeds since 2017. We believe that it will reduce Carbon Di-Oxide emissions in the Environment.
